About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated full-stack software engineer with a relentless drive to learn, grow, and excel in the world of technology. My journey into this field was inspired by my partner, who is a software engineer himself. Witnessing his expertise and the excitement he brings to his work, I became eager to join him on this captivating journey. Together, we aspire to embark on thrilling home projects that combine our skills and creative minds, allowing us to not only share our love for technology but also teach our children the art of programming as a united front. As a motivated team player, I thrive in collaborative environments, leveraging my keen eye for detail to catch even the most elusive bugs and ensure code integrity. Maintaining a positive outlook and cultivating an attitude of gratitude are essential components of my work ethic, empowering me to contribute effectively to team dynamics and tackle complex challenges with unwavering determination. Beyond the realm of coding, I find solace and joy in tending to my own garden, nurturing the ingredients that go into creating my cherished homemade salsa. My strong connection to Mexican cuisine, deeply rooted in my culture as a Latina, fuels my culinary passions. As a devoted mother of two, I am driven by the aspiration to work tirelessly and provide for my family, embodying resilience and determination in all aspects of my life.
